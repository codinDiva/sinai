About Sinai Solution

Why choose us?

Sinai Sensible Solution is an indigenous company, registered in Nigeria as one of the country’s Inspection Companies. Sinai is subsidiary of Fidtech Global Engineering Resources Limited, one of the foremost EPCI companies in Nigeria. The aim of establishing the company is to provide efficient and quality services to the Shipping, Oil/Petroleum and Agro Allied Industries both within and outside the country.

Sinai Sensitive Solution is registered with the Directorate of Petroleum Resources and has permit to operate as an oil/petrochemical servicing company in the area of Marine Services and Cargo Inspections. .
